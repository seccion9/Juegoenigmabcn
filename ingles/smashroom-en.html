SMASH ROOM

A Smash Room is a safe space designed to release stress and tension while having a great time. Here, you can break objects such as plates, glass and other items to let off steam.



Everything will be supervised in a safe environment where you can let off steam, whether on your own or with others, in an entertaining and liberating way. Hit, smash and destroy without worries. In our Smash Room, the most important thing is that you have fun and leave feeling satisfied.



Go ahead and try this incredible experience! Transform stress and anxiety into joy and calm, all while creating an unforgettable memory full of fun.


We’re waiting for you at the Smash Room, where letting go is the secret to achieving a lighter mind and a revitalized spirit!

What can I break?

There is a wide variety of objects to break: plates, bottles, discarded appliances, and more. All of them are prepared safely to ensure a controlled experience.

PROTECTION

Safety is a priority for us. We will provide you with protective equipment, including safety goggles, gloves and, on occasion, special suits to ensure a safe, injury-free experience.

PARTICIPANTS

This is an experience for adults over 18.

DURATIONN

Every Smash Room session lasts 30 minutes.

PRICING

Every session is up to 2 people.
