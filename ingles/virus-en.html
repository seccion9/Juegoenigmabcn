CHILDREN UNDER 13 MUST BE ACCOMPANIED

Although the mission is geared towards adults, younger children will have a blast and can participate from the age of 7, always with adult supervision. For all other cases, children must be accompanied by an adult: we recommend one adult per child for a more enjoyable experience.
